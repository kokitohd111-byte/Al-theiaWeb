"Succubus Blood"

“Succubus Blood” is an interactive visual novel in which the player enters a peaceful, seemingly quiet town. Motivated by his grandmother, the protagonist’s family decides to move far away from the noisy city.

What will happen in this town? What secrets does it hide?
What mysteries lie behind its inhabitants?
What destiny will you choose for the protagonist?
